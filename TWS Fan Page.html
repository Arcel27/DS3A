History

TWS—short for "Twenty Four Seven With Us"—is a six-member boy group under Pledis Entertainment. They officially debuted on January 22, 2024 with their first mini album Sparkling Blue and its title track “Plot Twist.”

Their music style, called boyhood pop, shares youthful emotions and the excitement of first love. The fandom is named 42 (SAI), meaning “24/7 with us,” symbolizing the connection between the group and fans.
